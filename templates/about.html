What is this?

Visit here to learn about what problem this app solves and how it works. Check out these articles to learn more about the forgetting curve and how the brain works.

  • https://uwaterloo.ca/campus-wellness/curve-forgetting
  • https://www.edapp.com/blog/ebbinghaus-forgetting-curve/

Why 2.7?

The default for the "event frequency" is 2.7. This means that the gap between the events increases by a multiple of 2.7 every time. The number e (approximately 2.7) seems to be the best number for destroying the forgetting curve. Though, you're always free to change it to what works best for you!
